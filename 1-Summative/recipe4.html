Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter (melted)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder

Instructions

    >Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a baking pan.
  1. Mix melted butter, sugar, and eggs in a bowl.
  2. Stir in cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Spread batter evenly in the pan.
  4. Bake for 20–25 minutes until set. Cool before cutting.
