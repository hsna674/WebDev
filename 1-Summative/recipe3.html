Spaghetti

Ingredients

    • 2 cups lettuce (chopped)
    • 1 tomato (diced)
    • 1/2 cucumber (sliced)
    • 1/4 red onion (thinly sliced)
    • 2 tbsp olive oil
    • 1 tbsp lemon juice
    • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

    1. Wash and prepare all vegetables.
    2. Combine lettuce, tomato, cucumber, and onion in a bowl.
    3. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper for the dressing.
    4. Pour dressing over salad and toss gently.
    5. Serve fresh and enjoy!
