Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Whisk in milk, egg, and melted butter until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly greased pan over medium heat.
  4. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles form on top, then flip.
  5. Cook the other side until golden brown and serve warm.
