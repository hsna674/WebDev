Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Boil water and cook spaghetti until tender.
  2. Heat sauce with olive oil.
  3. Drain pasta and mix with sauce.
  4. Serve with salt and pepper.
